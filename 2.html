RasenBallsport Leipzig e.V. (lit. 'Lawn Ball Sports Leipzig'),
commonly known as RB Leipzig or informally as Red Bull Leipzig, is a German professional football club based in Leipzig,

Saxony. The club was founded in 2009 by the initiative of the company Red Bull GmbH,
which purchased the playing rights of fifth-tier side SSV Markranstädt with the intent of advancing
the new club to the top-flight Bundesliga within eight years. The men's professional football club is
run by the spin-off organization RasenBallsport Leipzig GmbH.[1] RB Leipzig plays its
home matches at the Red Bull Arena.
